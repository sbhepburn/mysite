“People think of education as something they can finish.” -Isaac Asimov

Born in British Columbia, Canada 1988. Since I was an infant, I've been drawn to the mechanical and the digital. I got my first job when I was 13 years old at an airport and was paid in flight time with an instructor. Nothing could hold me back from learning all about technology and the technical. I am a strong believer in the idea that anyone can teach themselves anything. I'm self taught in many things like 3d modeling, cooking, pixel art, web design, programming, drawing, baking and flying drones. I love cats and dogs, playing couch co-op indie games and making pizza from scratch.
