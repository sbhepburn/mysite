Who I am

Born in British Columbia in 1988, drawn to the mechanical and the digital since I was an infant. I got my first job at 13, at an airport, paid in flight time with an instructor — which taught me early that the best compensation is learning something you can't stop thinking about.

I'm a believer — with receipts — that anyone can teach themselves anything. I'm self-taught in web development, 3D modeling, cooking, pixel art, drawing, baking, and flying drones. These days that same curiosity has me leading platform, operations, and AI initiative teams in regulated healthcare tech: an Azure-first, build-over-buy shop with 17+ active projects and 115+ business applications under my watch. I find the bottleneck and fix the system, not the symptom — equal parts Phoenix Project and stubborn optimism.

Off the clock: pizza and sourdough from scratch, couch co-op indie games, cats and dogs (don't make me choose), good coffee, and hiking the Oregon Cascades and Coast Range.

What I do

The short version: I take messy, ambitious problems and ship calm, working systems.

  • Full-Stack Development

    React, Node.js, SQL, and the unglamorous glue in between. I rebuilt my hands-on fluency from the ground up, on purpose — so I never have to architect things I can't build.

  • Cloud & DevOps

    Azure, Azure DevOps, Docker, CI/CD pipelines with real deployment gates. If a release needs a human ritual and crossed fingers, I automate the ritual.

  • AI Engineering

    Production tools on the Anthropic API — MCP, tool use, evaluation frameworks — inside the Claude Partner Network. I ship AI utilities people use daily, not demos that die in Slack.

  • Identity & Systems Architecture

    SSO and JIT provisioning, Okta, SCIM-style flows, secrets management, API management. The plumbing nobody notices until it's perfect — or until it isn't.

  • Platform & Team Leadership

    I lead multiple teams across a 17-project, 115-application portfolio. Theory of Constraints is my love language: find the bottleneck, fix the system.

  • Local Web Design

    Fast, modern, affordable websites for Yamhill County small businesses — designed and built by me, start to finish. More on that below.
